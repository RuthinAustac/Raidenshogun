About Raiden Shogun

Raiden Shogun, also known as Ei, is a character in the popular game Genshin Impact. She is the Electro Archon and ruler of the Inazuma region. As the embodiment of Eternity, she is known for her stoic and mysterious nature.

Raiden Shogun wields the Musou No Hitachi, a powerful weapon that is central to her character's lore. She is also known for her role as one of the Seven Archons in Teyvat.
